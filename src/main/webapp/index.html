StudyHub: Team 18 Summer CodeU Project

Are you a student and are always looking for a new place to do work? Don't you hate it when you settle down to do some work and there's no outlet? Or no seats left? Or it's too noisy? Or food too expensive?

This is StudyHub, a place where you can find reviews of study spots near a school of your choice. Never be unproductive again!

Find and connect with other students near you, and share tips and secret locations to study!

As a user, you can create an account, login, post new reviews, and read through other people's! Find a hub near you and learn more about its amenities.
